Characters of Noli Me Tangere

Image Name of the Character Description
Maria Clara Maria Clara, whose full name was Maria Clara de los Santos, was the fiancée of Crisostomo Ibarra and the daughter of Capitan Tiago. After Ibarra went back to San Diego, Maria Clara encountered a lot of opposition to their engagement.
Crisostomo Ibarra Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin, also known as Crisostomo Ibarra, was a young college student from San Diego. After his father passed away, he went back to his hometown to try to start a respectable school, but he encountered many challenges from the locals. After being implicated in a rebellion, he later left the town and assumed the name Simoun, a jeweler.
Padre Damaso The real father of Maria Clara was Padre Damaso, a former town curate in San Diego. The man attempted to keep his daughter and Crisostomo Ibarra apart for Maria Clara's sake by opposing their marriage.
Capitan Tiago Maria Clara's father, Capitan Tiago, was a prominent businessman in San Diego. Betrothing his daughter to Crisostomo Ibarra, Capitan Tiago struggled to obey the will of the friars.
Elias Elias was a wanted man who resided in San Diego. Enduring one tragedy after another, he began working to improve society. After his life was saved by Crisostomo Ibarra, he began aiding the young man, saving him on numerous occasions.
